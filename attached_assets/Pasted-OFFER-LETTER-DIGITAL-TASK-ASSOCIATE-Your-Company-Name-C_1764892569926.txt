2. Role Purpose

As a Digital Task Associate, you will perform daily digital activities within our platform. Your role directly contributes to improving digital engagement quality and operational efficiency.

3. Key Responsibilities

Your responsibilities include:
	•	Completing assigned digital tasks through the platform
	•	Following instructions for content-based or engagement-based activities
	•	Ensuring accuracy and timely completion of tasks
	•	Reporting task completion through the app
	•	Maintaining output quality in line with company standards
	•	Participating in occasional training or updates as required

(Your daily tasks will appear inside the app dashboard.)

⸻

4. Working Schedule

This role allows flexibility. Tasks can be completed at any time within the daily or weekly requirement, unless otherwise specified inside the system.

⸻

5. Performance & Earnings

Your performance is evaluated based on:
	•	Number of tasks completed
	•	Accuracy and consistency
	•	Following task guidelines

Earnings are based on the platform’s performance structure, which is automatically calculated within the app. Your balance will be visible in your dashboard.

No payment is requested upfront. Your earnings are released as per the platform’s weekly/monthly schedule.

⸻

6. Eligibility

To remain eligible for this role, you must:
	•	Maintain an active account
	•	Complete minimum required tasks
	•	Follow platform guidelines and community rules
